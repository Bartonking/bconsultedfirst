# Full Website Specification

## Project

**bConsulted First — Shopify Audit Website**

## Website Goal

Turn the current consultancy website into an **audit-led lead generation website** where the primary action is:

**Lead enters email + Shopify store URL → AI generates a preliminary audit → report is shown and emailed → lead is invited to book a consultation**

This website should do two jobs at once:

1.  generate trust and authority,
2.  convert visitors into qualified leads through the audit offer.

---

# 1. Strategic Direction

## Core Positioning

bConsulted First is a **Shopify operations consultancy** that helps growing ecommerce businesses reduce manual work, improve workflow reliability, and build stronger back-office systems.

## New Front-Door Offer

Instead of pushing discovery calls first, the site should lead with:

**Free AI-Powered Shopify Operations Audit**

This is not a full internal audit. It is a **preliminary operational snapshot** based on public storefront signals and structured AI analysis.

## Core Conversion Logic

The website should move users through this funnel:

### Stage 1 — Curiosity

“Something in my store operations may be slowing us down.”

### Stage 2 — Low-friction offer

“Get a free AI-powered preliminary audit.”

### Stage 3 — Value delivery

“Here are the key issues and opportunities we found.”

### Stage 4 — Sales transition

“Book a consultation for a deeper human-led audit.”

# 16. My Product Opinion on This Website Direction

This is the right move.

Your current site explains the business well, but this new structure will make the website **work harder**. Instead of asking visitors to trust you before receiving value, you give them value first through the audit, then convert them into consultation leads.

That is a much stronger website model.

My one warning is this:  
**keep the audit promise narrow and credible.**  
The site wins when the audit feels sharp, honest, and useful.
